## 1. Air Freight Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

### Customs Clearance Time

| Shipment Type | Hub Locations | Clearance Time |
|--------------|---------------|----------------|
| **Standard Air Freight** | TPE (Taipei), SGN (Ho Chi Minh), SIN (Singapore) | **12–24 hours** |
| **Express Shipments** | Same hubs | **4–6 hours** (if docs submitted ≥12h before arrival) |

**Required Documentation for Express:**
- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- House Air Waybill (HAWB)

### Importing Electronic Components

**Source countries:** Taiwan, South Korea → Southeast Asia

**Required documents:**
1. Commercial Invoice with exact **Manufacturer Part Number (MPN)**
2. Packing List showing **net and gross weights** per carton
3. Certificate of Origin (**Form D** or **Form E**) for tariff reductions
4. Import Permit from local Ministry of Industry and Trade (if applicable)

**Packaging requirement:** All electronic components must adhere to **anti-static ESD standards**.

## 2. Ocean Freight Policies (FCL & LCL)

### FCL vs LCL Processing

| Cargo Type | Route | Customs Timeline |
|------------|-------|------------------|
| **FCL** (Full Container Load) | Port-to-port | **2–3 business days** after vessel discharge |
| **LCL** (Less than Container Load) | Via CFS (Container Freight Station) | **+24–48 hours** for de-consolidation before customs |

### Demurrage & Detention Policy

| Fee Type | Start Day | Free Time | Standard Period |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Demurrage** | Container remains inside port terminal | 5 days after discharge | 5 days free |
| **Detention** | Empty container not returned to shipping line depot | 7 days after discharge | 7 days free |

> Demurrage = container stays in port terminal
> Detention = container not returned to depot

## 3. Dangerous Goods (DG) Protocols

### Shipping Lithium-Ion Batteries

**UN Classification:** UN3480 (cargo) / UN3481 (packed with equipment)

**Required documentation:**
1. **MSDS** (Material Safety Data Sheet) — updated within current calendar year
2. **UN38.3 Test Summary** — proving transport testing compliance
3. **Dangerous Goods Declaration (DGD)** — signed by certified packager

**Packaging requirements:**
- Display **Class 9 Hazard Label**
- Display **lithium battery handling label**

**Transport restrictions:**
- ❌ **Prohibited on passenger aircraft**
- ✅ Cargo Aircraft Only (CAO) or sea freight

## 4. Customs Clearance and Tariff Procedures

### Duty Calculation for Imported Machinery

**Basis:** CIF (Cost, Insurance, and Freight) value

**Calculation inputs:**
- **HS Code** (Harmonized System tariff code)
- **CIF value** of imported machinery
- Applicable duty rate

### Free Trade Agreement (FTA) Benefits

| Agreement | Routes | Duty Reduction | Form Required |
|-----------|--------|----------------|----------------|
| **ASEAN-China FTA** | Southeast Asia ↔ China | **10–15% → 0–5%** | Form E |

**Additional charges:**
- **VAT:** 10% applied to total duty-paid value

### Standard Duty Rates (Machinery)

| Scenario | Rate |
|----------|------|
| Standard (no FTA) | 10–15% |
| With Form E (ASEAN-China FTA) | 0–5% |
| VAT | 10% of duty-paid value |

## Quick Reference

### Key Form Numbers
- **Form D** — Certificate of Origin (ASEAN FTA)
- **Form E** — Certificate of Origin (ASEAN-China FTA)

### Key UN Numbers
- **UN3480** — Lithium ion batteries (cargo)
- **UN3481** — Lithium ion batteries (packed with equipment)

### Key Timeframes
- Air freight express clearance: **4–6 hours** (with advance docs)
- FCL ocean customs: **2–3 business days**
- LCL de-consolidation buffer: **+24–48 hours**
- Demurrage free time: **5 days**
- Detention free time: **7 days**
